Help — FAQs

How do I book a ticket?
Search for a train, open its booking page, choose class and seats, then proceed to payment.
Can I cancel a ticket?
Yes — open your booking and cancel. Refund rules depend on how early you cancel.
How do I order food?
After booking, use the Order Food panel (or visit Meal Options), enter your PNR and choose items.
What payment methods are supported?
The demo supports Card and UPI (simulated). In production, integrate a payment gateway.
Who can I contact for support?
Use the Contact page to send us a message or call the customer helpline shown on the login page.
