Meal Options & Ordering

Follow these steps to order meals onboard:

  1. Choose Train: Search and select the train you'll travel on.
  2. Enter PNR: Once you have a confirmed booking, note your PNR code.
  3. Select Meal: Browse meal categories and add items to your order.
  4. Confirm Order: Submit the order; you'll receive an order ID and status in Order History.

You can also view the live menu and place orders from the booking page or by visiting the API endpoint /menu.
